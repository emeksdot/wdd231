Site Plan for Mildred Online Sneakers Shop

Site Name: MILDRED ONLINE SNEAKERS SHOP (MOSS)

The site bears the name of my youngest child Serena Mildred. I have always been intrigued and inquisitive to know the internal workings of an ecommerce website such as Amazon, Alibaba, Jumia etc, and I feel this will be a good opportunity to experiment with pushing this idea to reality.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to give me a platform to reach out to visitors and customers to make purchases of their sports wears especially sneakers. Products will be displayed along with some information such as price, along with a form for a newsletter subscription.
